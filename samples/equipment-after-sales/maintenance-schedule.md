## Routine inspection

Inspect the equipment exterior, intake grille, outlet grille, display panel, and power cord at the start of each service visit. Confirm that the intake and outlet are clear before running the unit.

## Cleaning interval

Wipe exterior surfaces with a dry lint-free cloth every 30 operating days. Do not pour liquid into the intake, outlet, display panel, or power connector.

## Service log

Record the service date, unit serial label, filter status, observed fault code, and next recommended inspection date in the service log before returning the unit to the customer.
