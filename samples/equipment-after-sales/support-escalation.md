## Escalation package

When escalating a service case, include the serial label, installation location type, service log entry, fault code, filter age, photos of intake and outlet clearance, and customer contact window.

## Immediate escalation

Escalate immediately if there is burning smell, visible smoke, liquid entry, repeated E30 motor fault, damaged power cord, or any customer report of electrical shock.

## Customer message

Tell the customer to stop using the unit until support responds when an immediate escalation condition is present. Provide the case number and expected support response window.
