## No power

If the unit does not power on, confirm the outlet works with a known safe tester, confirm the power cord is fully seated, and check whether the display is blank or showing a fault code.

## Weak airflow

For weak airflow, check for blocked intake, blocked outlet, saturated filter, or operation in silent mode. Do not bypass the filter to increase airflow.

## Fault codes

For E12 sensor fault or E30 motor fault, stop customer operation and escalate to qualified support. Do not instruct a customer to open the motor housing or sensor module.
