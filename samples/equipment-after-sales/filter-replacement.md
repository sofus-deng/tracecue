## Replacement trigger

Replace the filter when the display shows FILTER-90, when airflow remains weak after exterior cleaning, or when the service log shows 90 operating days since the last filter replacement.

## Safe replacement steps

Power off the unit, unplug the power cord, wait two minutes, open the filter cover, remove the used filter, install one approved replacement filter, close the cover, and reconnect power.

## Reset check

After replacement, hold the filter reset button for five seconds. The FILTER-90 indicator should clear and normal airflow should resume within one minute.
