## Covered items

The limited warranty covers defects in the original fan motor, control board, and display module when the unit is used within documented operating limits.

## Not covered

Consumable filters, cosmetic wear, damage from liquid entry, unauthorized housing repair, non-approved replacement parts, and operation outside indoor dry-room limits are not covered.

## Review required

Do not promise warranty approval in the QR guide. Warranty decisions require support review of the serial label, service log, fault evidence, and operating conditions.
