## Power safety

Do not operate the unit with a damaged cord, wet hands, standing water nearby, or an extension cable that is not rated for the equipment load.

## Service boundary

Frontline staff may clean exterior surfaces, replace approved filters, inspect visible intake and outlet blockage, and record fault details. Frontline staff must not open sealed electrical, sensor, or motor housings.

## Operating limits

The unit is for indoor dry-room operation only. Stop operation if smoke, burning smell, liquid entry, repeated motor fault, or unusual vibration is observed.
